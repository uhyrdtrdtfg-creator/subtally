Subtally 1.0 — Initial Release

Thanks for installing Subtally! This first release includes:
- iCloud sync across all your devices, encrypted in your private database
- Live Activities and Dynamic Island for trial countdowns and renewals
- Home Screen widgets for monthly total, upcoming, and category views
- Webhook automation triggered by subscription events
- Vision OCR for receipt and invoice import
- Siri and Shortcuts integration via App Intents
- Family splitting for shared subscriptions
- On-device smart insights to surface idle subs and savings

Found something? Reach us via in-app Contact or https://subtally.app/support.
