Subtally — See every subscription. Cut every leak.

How many services do you actually pay for? What renews next week? Which free trial is about to silently turn into a charge? Subtally is a modern subscription manager for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch that puts every recurring expense in one private, beautifully designed place — with proactive reminders, smart insights, and zero tracking.

— Highlights —

iCloud Sync
Your data syncs seamlessly between your devices via CloudKit, stored encrypted in your private iCloud database. Switch devices without lifting a finger. Nothing ever touches a third-party server.

Live Activity & Dynamic Island
Trial countdowns and imminent renewals appear as Live Activities on the Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island, so the moments that matter never slip past you.

Home Screen Widgets
Multiple widget sizes for monthly total, upcoming renewals, and category breakdowns — see your subscription pulse without opening the app.

Webhook Automation
Trigger custom webhooks on subscription added, renewed, cancelled, or N days before a trial ends. Connect to Notion, Slack, Home Assistant, Shortcuts, or anything that speaks HTTP — and weave your subscription state into your real workflow.

Vision OCR
Snap a receipt, invoice, or confirmation email. The built-in Apple Vision OCR pipeline extracts amount, date, merchant, and renewal cycle, then imports it with a single tap. No more manual entry.

Siri & Shortcuts
Deep App Intents integration. Ask Siri "how much do I still owe in subscriptions this month" or "when is my next renewal", and chain Subtally into Shortcuts (e.g. payday digest, end-of-month summary).

Family Splitting
Share a subscription across multiple people and split the cost proportionally — perfect for couples, roommates, and families sharing streaming and cloud storage.

Smart Insights
Fully on-device analytics (your data never leaves the device) surface actionable suggestions: idle subscriptions, annual vs monthly comparisons, foreign exchange swings, year-over-year heat maps, and anonymous peer benchmarks.

— Privacy First —
Everything is stored locally and in your private iCloud. We collect no personal data, run no trackers, serve no ads, and sell nothing. A full PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy manifest is shipped with the app.
